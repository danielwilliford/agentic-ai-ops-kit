# Homeowners Water Damage SOP

Water damage claims require source-backed review before any coverage recommendation. The adjuster or reviewer must verify claim identifier, loss type, reported timeline, policy section, and exclusions.

Required evidence:

- claim identifier
- date or sequence of reported events
- water source category if available
- relevant homeowners policy section
- exclusions or escalation triggers

Human approval is required before final coverage decision, payment authorization, denial, or customer communication.
